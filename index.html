THE GALLERY

P0RTF0LI0 is a gallery based in Lyon. Opened in 2023, our gallery is founded on a passion for photography.

The last two decades have seen photography grow from a marginalised art form to one of the mainstays of the contemporary art world.

The development of the local scene matter a lot for us, we aim to help and see Lyon's artists grow.

Photography constantly pushes the boundaries that define it, and as such we are constantly delighted and challenged by the artists we work with.
